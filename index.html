GEU ACM-W

GEU ACM-W Student Chapter is reserved exclusively for women with an aim to highlight the fact that women are as competent as men in the field of technology.

To achieve this, we organize various activities and events to bring out the programmer and genius within each and every individual.

about us

Looking for Technical Stuff

GEU ACM-W seeks to take forward the task of the ACM community, but with a particular focus on the empowerment of women in computing. ACM-W Student Chapter is created to educate women about careers in computing and provide networking opportunities.

ACM-W supports, celebrates, and advocates internationally for the full engagement of women in all aspects of the computing field, providing a wide range of programs and services to ACM members and working in the larger community to advance the contributions of technical women.

Our Divisions

Management Team

The Management team is responsible for the overall management of the chapter activities, including the organization of the events, seminars and workshops.

Technical Team

The Technical Team is the backbone of our chapter, it is not only associated with programming, it works accross projects, learning, teaching & sloving problems.

Editorial Team

The Editorial Team is responsible for handling and maintaining all the documentations and preparing the report covering all the details of the event.

Design Team

The Design Team,one of the most important pillar, is responsible for making the posters, certificates and magazines for the events and the Chapter activities.

Marketing Team

The Marketing Team is responsible for the communication and pitching of the event among the students for their active participation in the Chapter activities.

Social Media Team

The Social Media Team is responsible for posting the details of the event in various social media platforms and also the winners of the respective activity.
